Cat Cafes Around The U.S. : An Alternative Model for Rescued Cat Adoption

Cat cafes were popularized in Japan but they have made their way to the U.S. in recent years, much to the glee of all cat-obsessed Americans. And what's great is that the majority of these cafes are very much focused on the well-being of the cats in their establishment, and serve as an alternative model for rescued cat adoption.
